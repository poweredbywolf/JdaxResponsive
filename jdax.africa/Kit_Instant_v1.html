WHAT ARE DIGITAL ASSETS?
Digital assets is a formal term for crypto-currencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Ripple.
SUPPORTED DIGITAL ASSETS
JDAX exchange currently supports 5 digital currencies: Bitcoin, Ethereum, Ripple, Bitcoin Cash and Litecoin.
WHAT IS A WALLET?
This is where you buy, send, receive and store digital currency.
❮ ❯
Select WALLETS from the menu, and then select the WALLET of the currency you want to sell.
GO TO YOUR WALLET
