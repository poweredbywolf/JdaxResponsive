THROUGH BANK TRANSFER
GO TO YOUR ZAR WALLET
We accept deposits from any bank account in the world. If you are sending through one of South Africa’s four major banks (ABSA, Standard Bank, Nedbank or FNB) then your deposit should reflect immediately. (Other banks: same day transfer?)
If you want to buy digital assets, you will first need to make a deposit into your ZAR Wallet. You can find your wallet from the MENU, select WALLETS and then your ZAR WALLET.
SELECT YOUR BANK
You can deposit a minimum of R200 upto unlimited amount. There will be no commission fee for depositing via bank transfer.
ENTER AMOUNT
Your unique reference number and all the relevant banking details are then displayed. Using these details, make the EFT from your bank. Double check your reference number – we use it to direct the funds the ZAR wallet on your account.
GO MAKE DEPOSIT WITH DETAILS PROVIDED
THROUGH DEBIT / CREDIT CARD
GO TO YOUR ZAR WALLET
To make your deposits via cards for the first time, you need to link your card to your account. Follow the steps on the screen and enter your card details. Your card will then be confirmed by your bank. Our payment card verification usually takes less than an hour. Once your card has been verified, it will be available on the drop-down list for a quick access for further deposits. You can add several payment cards to your profile.
If you want to buy digital assets, you will first need to make a deposit into your ZAR Wallet. You can find your wallet from the MENU, select WALLETS and then your ZAR WALLET.
SELECT YOUR CARD
You can deposit a minimum of R20 upto R50,000. There will be a commission fee (3.5% + R 2.00) for depositing via credit card.
ENTER AMOUNT
When you confirm, the amount will immediately be deposited to your ZAR wallet, without having to go and make
a deposit from your bank separately.

WHAT IS FIAT?
Fiat refers to traditional currencies like
US dollars, British Pounds and South African Rands. The name stems from the fact that these currencies are issued through a formal act of government – a decree, also known as a fiat.
SUPPORTED FIAT CURRENCIES
At present, the only fiat crrency accommodated on the JDAX exchange is South African Rands.
WHAT IS A ZAR WALLET?
This is where you store your local currency (ZAR). If you want to buy digital assets, you will first need to make a deposit into your ZAR Wallet. You can find your wallet from the MENU, select WALLETS and then your ZAR WALLET.
